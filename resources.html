Because We Care

National AIDS Control Organization (NACO)

Formed in 1992, the National AIDS Control Organization (NACO) is India's forefront agency battling HIV/AIDS. It devises and executes nationwide strategies encompassing prevention, treatment, and support services. Through awareness campaigns and accessible healthcare, NACO aims to combat HIV/AIDS and improve the well-being of affected individuals.

Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR)

Founded in 1911, the Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) is a leading biomedical research institution in India. It conducts and coordinates medical research to tackle various health issues, including infectious and non-communicable diseases, and collaborates globally to enhance healthcare advancements and outcomes within the country.

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) website on sexually transmitted infections (STIs) offers a comprehensive resource for information on prevention, testing, treatment, and statistics. It provides valuable educational materials and tools to promote awareness and support efforts to reduce the spread of STIs in communities nationwide.

STDCheck.com

STDCheck.com is a reputable online service providing confidential STD testing nationwide. With a user-friendly interface, individuals can easily select and order tests online. They then visit a local lab for sample collection and receive results electronically within days. This platform offers convenience, privacy, and peace of mind, empowering individuals to take control of their sexual health discreetly and efficiently from the comfort of their homes.
